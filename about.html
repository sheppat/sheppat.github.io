About Nicholas & UsefulUtilities

Hello! I'm Nicholas, a 16-year-old Roblox enthusiast, developer, and creator based in Oshawa, Canada. My passion is building tools, games, and utilities to make Roblox fun, engaging, and safe for everyone.

My journey began with curiosity about how Roblox works and how games are designed. Over time, I’ve learned JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Firebase integration to create reliable tools that provide stats, analytics, and enhanced gameplay experiences.

UsefulUtilities is my platform to share these creations with the Roblox community. Each tool and game is designed with privacy, safety, and usability in mind. Players can track stats, explore games, and access tools without sharing personal information.

Mission Statement: Our mission is to help Roblox players have fun, learn, and grow through safe, easy-to-use tools and games. We focus on innovation, community engagement, and educational gaming experiences.

At UsefulUtilities, we believe in:

  • Creating safe and enjoyable experiences for Roblox players of all ages.
  • Building tools that provide real value, insights, and entertainment.
  • Listening to the community and improving continuously.
  • Maintaining user privacy and data security as a top priority.

For suggestions, collaboration, advertising, or legal requests, feel free to reach me at usefulutilities.shop@gmail.com.
